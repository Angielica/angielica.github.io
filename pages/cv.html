About me

Angelica Liguori is a researcher at the Institute for High Performance Computing and Networking - National Research Council of Italy (ICAR-CNR). She received the Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) from the University of Calabria (UNICAL), Italy, in 2024. Her research interests include machine and deep learning. Specifically, she is interested in developing solutions in the area of Anomaly Detection/Generation in data sets.
